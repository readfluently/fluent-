Grade 10–12

FET Fluent focuses on advanced reading, subject-linked texts, oral preparation, digital literacy, and academic confidence. This phase supports learners as they read more deeply, speak more formally, and prepare for senior school demands.

Reading

📚 Library

Browse and open FET books, including literature, curriculum-linked support texts, digital literacy readers, and advanced reference books.

🗣️ Read Fluently

Guided reading support for older learners with passage reading, flow reading, reflection prompts, and meaning-focused fluency practice.

📖 Reference Books

Open the reference shelf for dictionary support, writing guides, study support, and future exam preparation resources.

💻 Digital Literacy Books

Read texts on technology, digital thinking, professional communication, and modern learning topics relevant to senior learners.

Language Blocks and Sound Maps do not appear in FET. Those tools belong in Foundation and Intermediate phases. FET reading focuses on analysis, understanding, subject literacy, oral confidence, and academic application.

Learner Activities

🧩 Interactive Posters

Explore FET subject posters and visual study supports for Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Geography, History, Agricultural Sciences, and other senior topics.

🎯 Thinking Activities

Strengthen reasoning, interpretation, comparison, analysis, and problem solving through more mature learner activities.

🎤 Oral Preparation

Prepare speeches, presentations, formal oral tasks, and academic speaking exercises with support for structure, practice, and confidence.

✍️ Writing and Response Tools

Build stronger written responses, essay thinking, and structured academic answers through senior-phase writing support tools.
