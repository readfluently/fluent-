Grade 7–9

Senior Phase Fluent focuses on reading with understanding, stronger oral confidence, subject-linked activities, and meaningful participation in events and competitions.

Reading

📚 Library

Browse and open books for Senior Phase reading, including fiction, curriculum-linked texts, digital literacy books, and reference books.

🗣️ Read Fluently

Guided reading practice and fluency support for older learners, with paragraph reading, flow reading, stop-and-think moments, and meaning-focused reading support.

📖 Reference Books

Open the reference shelf for support books such as the dictionary and future study or writing guides.

Language Blocks and Sound Maps do not appear in Senior Phase. Those tools belong in Foundation and Intermediate phases, while Senior Phase focuses on understanding, interpretation, oral growth, and subject-linked literacy.

Learner Activities

🧩 Interactive Posters

Explore grade- and subject-appropriate posters for Geography, Natural Sciences, History, and other Senior Phase topics.

🎯 Thinking Activities

Develop reasoning, interpretation, comparison, and academic confidence through mature learner activities.

🎤 Oral Preparation

Prepare speeches, oral presentations, and classroom speaking tasks with tools that support organisation, practice, and confidence.

Events and Competitions

Explore quizzes, poster showcases, map skills events, and other academic competitions linked to subjects.
