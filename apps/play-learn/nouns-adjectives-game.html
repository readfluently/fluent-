Grammar Game

Nouns and Adjectives

This board game helps learners practise nouns, adjectives, and pronouns through play. It includes common nouns, proper nouns, abstract nouns, collective nouns, descriptive adjectives, and simple pronoun questions.

Quick lesson

A noun names a person, place, thing, idea, or feeling. An adjective describes a noun. A pronoun replaces a noun so we do not repeat it too often.

How to play

  • 2–4 players can play.
  • All players start at START.
  • Roll the dice and move forward.
  • Read the instruction aloud and answer it.
  • If the answer is wrong, move back one square.
  • The first player to reach the final square wins.
