Nouns and Adjectives

Roll the dice, move across the board, listen to the question, and record your spoken answer.

Quick lesson

A noun names a person, place, thing, idea, or feeling. An adjective describes a noun. A pronoun replaces a noun so we do not repeat it too often.

Common nouns

  • boy
  • school
  • dog
  • book
  • city

Proper nouns

  • Sipho
  • Cape Town
  • Monday
  • Africa
  • Netflix

Abstract nouns

  • courage
  • love

Collective nouns

  • a team of players
  • a class of learners
  • a herd of cows
  • a bunch of keys
  • a pack of cards

How to play

  • 2 players can play.
  • Roll the dice and move forward.
  • The landed square is read aloud automatically.
  • The player records a spoken answer.
  • If the answer is wrong, move back one square.
  • The first player to reach the finish wins.
