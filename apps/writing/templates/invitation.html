Invitation

Write an invitation to an event. Include the important details clearly and use a polite tone.

Writing guide

Check the purpose, structure, and language features before you begin.

Purpose

To invite someone to an event or activity.

Text structure
  • Name of invitee
  • Nature of the event
  • Date and time
  • Place or venue
  • Optional dress code
  • Optional RSVP
Language features
  • Can be formal or informal
  • Short and clear
  • Uses polite invitation phrases
  • Response should be polite if included
Task

Write an invitation to a school reading celebration. Invite a parent, guardian, or friend to attend.

Helpful phrases

I would like to invite you to...
Please join us for...
The event will take place at...
We hope to see you there.

Checklist
  • Did I say what the event is?
  • Did I include the date and time?
  • Did I say where it will happen?
  • Is my invitation polite and clear?

    Exemplar invitation

    Dear Mrs Mokoena

    I would like to invite you to our Grade 5 Reading Celebration. The event will take place on Friday, 21 June 2026 at 14:00 in the school hall. Learners will share poems, short readings, and book displays. Smart casual dress is welcome. Please RSVP to the class teacher by Wednesday, 19 June. We hope you will be able to join us for this special afternoon.

    Yours sincerely
    Faith
