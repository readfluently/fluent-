Advertisement / Poster / Notice

Create a message that informs or persuades clearly and catches the reader’s attention.

Writing guide

Use strong wording, clear details, and an eye-catching message.

Purpose

To persuade someone to buy something, attend something, or notice important information.

Text structure
  • Strong heading or slogan
  • Main message
  • Important details
  • Optional contact information, date, or venue
  • Can include a visual idea
Language features
  • Short and memorable language
  • Persuasive words
  • Repetition, rhyme, or alliteration if useful
  • Design and layout matter
Task

Create a poster to advertise your school reading club for new members.

Helpful ideas

Join the fun!
Read more, learn more!
Every Tuesday after school
School library
All readers welcome

Checklist
  • Did I use a strong heading?
  • Did I make the message clear?
  • Did I include key details?
  • Does the writing sound persuasive or informative?
