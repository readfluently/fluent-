Procedures

Write clear instructions to explain how something is done step by step.

Writing guide

Include a goal, materials, and steps in the correct order.

Purpose

To describe or instruct how something is done through sequenced steps.

Text structure
  • Title or goal
  • Materials or equipment needed
  • Steps in order
  • Optional visual support
Language features
  • Use the imperative: cut, fold, place, mix
  • Use sequence words: first, next, then, finally
  • Be clear and direct
  • May use numbers or bullet points
Task

Write a procedure explaining how to make a simple bookmark for reading time.

Helpful starter

Goal: How to make a bookmark
Materials: coloured paper, scissors, ruler, crayons, glue

Checklist
  • Did I state what is being made?
  • Did I list the materials clearly?
  • Are my steps in the correct order?
  • Did I use clear action words?

    Exemplar procedure

    Goal: How to make a bookmark

    Materials: coloured paper, ruler, scissors, crayons, glue

    First, take a strip of coloured paper and measure it with a ruler. Next, cut the paper into a long rectangle. Then decorate the bookmark with crayons or patterns. After that, write your name on the back. Finally, punch a small hole at the top and add a short ribbon if you want. Your bookmark is now ready to use in your reading book.
