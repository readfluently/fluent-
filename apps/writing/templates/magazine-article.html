Magazine Article

Write an interesting article that informs the reader in a lively and engaging way.

Writing guide

Use an attractive heading, descriptive writing, and a style that speaks to the reader.

Purpose

To inform, educate, enlighten, and entertain the public.

Text structure
  • An attractive and interesting heading
  • Important details such as names, places, and times
  • Longer paragraphs with descriptive information
  • Possible quotes and direct address to the reader
Language features
  • More personal than a newspaper article
  • Can mix formal and informal language
  • Can use rhetorical questions
  • Uses descriptive and emotive language
Task

Write a magazine article about why reading is an exciting hobby for learners.

Helpful starter

Why do so many learners love reading?
Imagine stepping into a different world...
Books can help readers...

Checklist
  • Did I write an interesting heading?
  • Did I speak to the reader in an engaging way?
  • Did I include descriptive details?
  • Does my article keep the reader interested?

Teacher-style feedback

    Exemplar article

    Why Reading Is an Adventure

    Have you ever opened a book and felt as if you had stepped into another world? Reading is much more than just looking at words on a page. It is a way to explore exciting places, meet interesting characters, and discover new ideas. Some books make readers laugh, while others teach important facts about animals, space, or history. Reading also helps learners improve their vocabulary and become stronger writers. Best of all, there is a book for every kind of reader. Whether you enjoy mystery, sport, fantasy, or true stories, reading can become a fun and rewarding hobby that stays with you for life.
