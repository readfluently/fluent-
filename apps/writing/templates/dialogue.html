Dialogue Writing

Write a dialogue between two or more people. Use the correct format, punctuation, and clear speaking turns.

Writing guide

Use speaker names, correct punctuation, and natural conversation.

Purpose

To show a conversation clearly between characters or people.

Text structure
  • Write the speaker’s name followed by a colon
  • Start a new line for each new speaker
  • Keep the conversation clear and logical
  • Make each speaker sound natural
Language features
  • Use speech punctuation correctly
  • Use suitable tone for the situation
  • Keep the dialogue connected to the topic
  • Check spelling and grammar carefully
Task

Write a dialogue between two learners who are choosing a book from the library.

Helpful starter

Lerato: Which book do you want to choose?
Thabo: I am not sure yet...
Lerato: Let us look at...

Checklist
  • Did I use speaker names?
  • Did I start a new line for each speaker?
  • Does the conversation make sense?
  • Did I check punctuation, spelling, and grammar?
