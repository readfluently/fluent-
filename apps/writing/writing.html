What learners will do

The Writing Studio is designed to support independent writing first, followed by guided reflection.

In Picture-Guided Writing

Learners study an image, read the task, and write their own paragraph in a focused writing space. After submitting, they can compare their work with a guideline and exemplar response.

Observe the image Plan ideas Write independently Compare with exemplar

In Transactional & Response Writing

Learners choose a text type, use the writing template to check structure and language requirements, and then submit their work for teacher-style feedback on form, spelling, and grammar.

Check the purpose Follow the structure Write on a topic Review feedback

What support is built in

The page is designed to feel supportive without doing the writing for the learner.

No autocorrect while typing

Learners write freely and think for themselves first. Corrections are shown only after they choose to submit their work.

Clear writing expectations

Each task or template gives the learner a clear sense of what the writing should include, how it should be organised, and which language features matter.

Self-assessment and reflection

Exemplar responses, checklists, and teacher-style comments help learners compare, improve, and gradually mark their own work with confidence.

Fluent Writing Studio helps learners plan, write, reflect, and improve through guided practice.
