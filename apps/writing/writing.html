Transactional Writing

Open a writing template and practise using the correct purpose, audience, and format.

🗞️ Newspaper Article Write a report with a headline, facts, and organised paragraphs. 📖 Magazine Article Write an engaging article that informs and interests the reader. 💌 Invitation Create an invitation with the correct details and format. 📣 Advertisement / Poster / Notice Design short persuasive or informative texts for different audiences. 💬 Dialogue Write a conversation using the correct format and punctuation. 📝 Personal Recount Retell a personal experience in a clear and interesting sequence. 📋 Procedures Write instructions in the correct order using clear action steps. ⭐ Review Share your opinion about a book, film, event, or experience. 🧭 Giving Directions Explain clearly how to get from one place to another.

Picture Prompts Writing

Choose a picture-based writing activity. You can connect these buttons to your picture prompt pages.

🖼️ Picture Prompt 1 Write a story based on a single interesting picture. 🌄 Picture Prompt 2 Describe the setting, characters, and action shown in the image. 👣 Picture Prompt 3 Use the picture to plan a narrative with a beginning, middle, and end. 🎒 Picture Prompt 4 Write from the point of view of a character in the picture. 🐾 Picture Prompt 5 Use clues in the image to infer what happened before and after.
Writing tip Start by noticing the purpose of the task. Then think about who will read it and which details will make your writing clear and interesting.
